What is NDA?

The National Defence Academy (NDA) is India's joint services academy where cadets of the Army, Navy, and Air Force train together.

Eligibility & Exam Pattern

  • Age: 16.5 to 19.5 years
  • Gender: Male & Female
  • Education: Passed 12th class
